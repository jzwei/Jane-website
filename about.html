Hello! My name is Jane Zweibel. I am a Philadelphia based accessories designer with overflowing love for curly dogs, and making things. I am a Colorado native with a BA in International Business from the American University in Paris and an MS in Fashion Design from Drexel University. While I am not at home with my dog Frances, I'm at work (with my dog Frances) in the Philadelphia Naval Yard at Anthropologie Home's headquarters, where I am the Product Manager for Home Textiles. I am an avid maker of many things, including stained glass, outerwear, evening wear, embroidery, and this website. Please visit my shop website (little-bones.com) for more!
